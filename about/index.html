Muckross Riding Stables


~ Bed & Breakfast ~


About us


Farmhouse set in a peaceful valley. Large warm comfortable rooms with panoramic views of the mountains, oak forest with red deer. Our area is considered a walkers paradise and of course perfect for riding. Short stroll to Muckross House and the Killarney Lakes.Pet farm avaible to gusts staying and pony rides avaible for small children


Muckross Riding Stables offer a scenic location within Killarney National Park and close to its lovely lakes. This property has horses and ponies on site to view and interact with. Muckross Riding Stables offers charming, spacious rooms. All rooms have heating, a bathroom and a flat-screen TV. The property also arranges cart trips around the park. Killarney has a beautiful setting, ideal for hill walking and cycling. The centre of Killarney is less than 4 km from the stables, while Tralee and the coast can both be reached in around 30 minutes by car.
